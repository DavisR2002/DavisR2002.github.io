What is Cyber Security?


If you've looked around within the last 20 years, you might have noticed that computers are commonplace nowadays: in your schools, shops and homes alike. Most people would sooner realize that they have forgotten their mobile phones (which are also computer systems) at home, than their wallets or bank cards.

With computers and computer systems being so ubiquitous in our everyday lives, it is no surprise that the media is often filled with stories of 'cyber-attacks' or 'data breaches' being carried out against companies and their customers.

As such, it stands to reason that there should be something in place in order to protect our treasured devices against those who would bring harm to them, whether physically or from afar. Cyber-Security is that something.

The word 'security' can be used to describe the protection and prevention of unauthorised access or damage against the protected object, whether that be a location, an item or a person.

Cyber-Security takes a more specialised approach to this concept, in that it is a term used to describe the protection of computer systems from threats. Information Security refers to a broader scope of protection, considering the more physical threats to computer systems.

This website has been created to serve as a guide for all things Digital Safety. Irrespective of if you are in need of a refresher, or are completely new to these concepts, this website will (hopefully) provide for you, reader, a simplified explanation of key concepts and practices that you or those around you can employ in order to be more educated in the digital world.
