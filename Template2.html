Key Points:


1. Cyber-Security serves to keep your information safe, allowing you to avoid expensive attacks (discussed in further sections.)

2. CIA refers to

  • Confidentiality
  • Integrity
  • Availabilty

    • Assessments and controls are put in place to examine and preserve the CIA Triad.

    3. Assets are things of value to a company. These can be physical, digital or even intangible. Attacks are an attempt attempt to damage or steal assets, in one of many ways.
